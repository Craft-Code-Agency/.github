# Craft + Code

[Craft + Code](https://craftandcode.agency) is a digital marketing and web design agency built for service businesses that want to grow online. We specialize in websites, SEO, paid advertising, and branding — the four things that actually move the needle for local and regional service companies.

We're based in Orlando, FL and work with service businesses across the country.

---

## What We Do

**Web Design & Development**
Fast, clean, conversion-focused websites built to generate leads — not just look good. Every site is built with SEO and performance in mind from day one.

**Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**
We help service businesses rank above their competitors in local and organic search. That includes technical SEO, on-page optimization, local SEO, and content strategy.

**Paid Media & Advertising**
Meta ads, Google ads, and paid campaigns built around one goal: qualified leads. We handle strategy, creative, and optimization.

**Branding**
Identity systems that make service businesses look like they mean it — logos, visual language, and brand guidelines that hold up across every touchpoint.
